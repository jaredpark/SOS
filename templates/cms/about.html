About Us

Kirby Ramirez, owner-operator of SOS Pool Rescue, LLC, was born in Santa Fe, NM and attended ASU in Tempe. He worked for Paddock Pools as an equipment and chemical specialist for 5 years during college.

Kirby has one son named Kielan W Ramirez. They both love sports and Kirby is very involved with Kielan's sports activities. Kirby is customer-oriented and he understands the value of giving good, reliable service and repairs to his customers. Go Sun Devils!

Sheila Ramirez, office Mom, came to Arizona to help Kirby set up his own business.

Her past customer service businesses include a courier business and a copying and duplicating business, both based in Santa Fe, NM. Her hobbies include tennis, pickleball, and traveling with her grandson.
